CyGame

CyGame is a turn based board game similar to Monopoly, and it's designed for two players. The goal is to either accumulate a set amount of money, or bankrupt the other player. Each player starts at square 0 with a starting cash balance. The players move clockwise around a board of any given length. The board consists of different types of squares, each with unique effects: Endzone grants prize money when passed, CyTown can be bought as a property to charge double rent, Pay Rent forces the player to pay the other player rent, Fall Behind moves the player back one square, Blizzard freezes the player until they hit an odd number, and finally Pass Class moves the player forward four squares. Pass Class can only happen once per turn per player.
